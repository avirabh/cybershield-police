# CyberShield Police Demo Video Script

## 2-3 Minute Flow

**0:00-0:15 - Opening**  
This is CyberShield Police, built for Prakasam Police Hackathon 2026, Mission Y4, Challenge 05: Cybercrime Detection and Digital Fraud. It is an AI-powered cybercrime triage platform for safer digital communities.

**0:15-0:30 - Homepage**  
Show the homepage, role cards, AI status, and demo account section. Say: "The system supports Citizen, Police Officer, and Admin/SP roles. All data is synthetic and demo-safe."

**0:30-0:45 - Citizen Login**  
Click Continue as Citizen. Open the citizen dashboard and reporting flow.

**0:45-1:05 - Citizen Report**  
Submit a suspicious message: "FAKE DEMO: Your bank KYC is blocked. Enter OTP and UPI PIN to reactivate." Show risk score, scam category, triggered risk factors, and police triage priority.

**1:05-1:25 - URL/UPI Scam Validator**  
Open the phishing scanner. Scan `secure-sbi-kyc-login.xyz` with KYC text. Show Critical/Phishing, local factors, URL reputation, and AI/Local explanation.

**1:25-1:45 - Transaction Monitor**  
Open Transaction Monitor. Analyze a fake loan processing fee or QR refund transaction. Show suspected fraud type, risk score, and recommended action.

**1:45-2:00 - CyberDost**  
Ask: "My friend asked me to send Rs. 5000 immediately. What should I do?" Show guidance, 1930 reminder, and multilingual/AI fallback support.

**2:00-2:30 - Police Dashboard**  
Login as Police Officer. Show police dashboard, high-priority cases, category analytics, recent cases, and case details with evidence metadata and summary.

**2:30-2:50 - Admin/SP Intelligence**  
Login as Admin/SP or open intelligence pages. Show hotspots, synthetic threat stream, indicators, and case pattern intelligence.

**2:50-3:00 - Closing**  
CyberShield Police is prevention-focused, explainable, multilingual, privacy-safe, and ready for local police hackathon demonstration.
