# Practical Social Impact

CyberShield Police focuses on early scam prevention, explainable triage, and safer police workflows.

## Citizen Benefits

- Citizens can paste suspicious SMS, WhatsApp, email, call notes, links, screenshots, or transaction notes before acting.
- Senior citizens get simple guidance for OTP, UPI refund, fake customer care, and digital arrest scams.
- Students and job seekers get warnings about fake jobs, registration fees, loan fees, and exam/certificate phishing.
- Multilingual UI support improves accessibility for Andhra Pradesh citizens while retaining English police summaries.

## Police Benefits

- Risk score, category, triage priority, and matched rules help officers prioritize high-risk cases.
- Dashboard analytics show category trends, high-priority queues, platform distribution, risk levels, and transaction risk.
- Hotspot analytics can guide awareness drives in locations showing synthetic high-risk concentration.
- Threat intelligence and pattern clustering demonstrate how repeated domains, scripts, receiver placeholders, or scam types could support early public warnings.

## Screenshot Analysis Impact

Many citizens do not know how to copy text from a scam message or fake page. The screenshot analyzer gives a safe fallback:

- stores only filename/type/size/time metadata by default
- allows pasted visible text for local analysis
- does not identify faces or people
- does not send images externally unless optional AI is explicitly enabled
